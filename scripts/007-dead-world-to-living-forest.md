# Script #7: Dead World to Living Forest

**Format:** Before/After
**Duration:** ~42 seconds
**Anti-slop score:** 38/50

## Hook Variants

**Variant A (visual-first):** Slow-mo of troll walking through reactive grass, reversed
**Variant B:** "My troll lived on a gray plane. Watch what happens next."
**Variant C:** Quick split — flat ground LEFT, lush forest RIGHT — smash cut

## Script (Dual-Track)

| VISUAL | VOICEOVER |
|--------|-----------|
| Slow-mo: troll walks through tall grass, every blade bends and springs back. Reverse. | *(no voiceover — pure visual hook)* |
| Cut to flat gray ground from old videos. Troll stands on nothing. | So I'm making a game about a Troll. And this is what his world looked like. |
| Quick flash of the hand-drawn level map | Time to fix that. |
| Fast montage: mountains rise from flat ground, river cuts through, cliffs form, paths connect. 3-4 sec timelapse. | *(no voiceover — let the montage breathe)* |
| Troll exits cave. Camera follows him across the new landscape — mountains, river, bridge visible. Bare terrain, no vegetation yet. | Okay. That's a world. But it's... empty. |
| Quick sequence: grass patches drop onto bare hills, flowers pop into meadows, ferns appear along the river, trees fall from sky into place — like placing pieces on a board. Fast, rhythmic, almost playful. | *(no voiceover — visual rhythm carries it)* |
| Wide shot: the same landscape, now covered in green. Full. Alive. | Better. |
| Troll walks on the grass. Grass doesn't move. Static. Dead. | But watch the grass. |
| Quick flash: RDR2 grass reacting to a horse. Ghost of Tsushima wind through fields. | I always loved when games care about this stuff. |
| Troll walks through grass again — grass explodes wildly in wrong direction | So let's make it real. |
| Second broken attempt — grass clips through troll's feet | Okay... not that real. |
| Final result: troll walks through — grass bends perfectly, ferns sway, bushes part. Slow-mo of the detail. | *(beat)* Yeah. That's the vibe. |
| Troll walks away through the reactive forest. Wide shot of the whole level. | Now something needs to live here. |

## Title Options

## Thumbnail Concept

Split-screen — left: troll on flat gray ground (old). Right: troll in lush forest with reactive grass bending under feet. Sharp diagonal cut.
